Future with will

Se usa para decisiones espontáneas, promesas, predicciones y hechos futuros inevitables.

Estructura

  • Afirmativa: Sujeto + will + verbo base → I will study, She will be happy
  • Negativa: Sujeto + will not + verbo → won't → They won't arrive
  • Interrogativa: Will + sujeto + verbo? → Will you help me?

Usos principales

  • Decisión espontánea: “I’m thirsty — I will get some water.”
  • Promesas/ofertas: “I will help you.”
  • Predicciones: “I think it will rain later.”
  • Futuro inevitable: “People will live longer.”

Will vs. Be going to

Decisión ahora: will → “I will carry it.”

Plan previo / evidencia: going to → “I am going to visit Bogotá.”

TIP: Con verbos de estado (think, believe, know) se usan will para predicciones.

Errores comunes

  • ❌ He will goes → ✅ He will go
  • ❌ I won't to go → ✅ I won't go

Mini‑quiz

Pregunta: Juan está sediento y decide beber agua. ¿Cuál es la forma correcta para expresar su acción futura?
